USAA Quote Flow

Small Business Insurance

Small business insurance at USAA is USAA’s first new insurance product in nearly 40 years. I was part of the dev team that was assembled to develop the user experience for the product. The project was cutting edge for USAA and involved interfacing with never before used backend and sass platforms. The project went from inception to launching its first product in less than 9 months. We operated in an MVP test and learn approach to development. We worked with our business partners to develop the minimum viable product and then learned from data gathered while users interacted with the experience. The program is considered a success within the company and continues to grow.

USAA Small Business Insurance Quote Flow

This is the quote flow for the small business insurance product at USAA. I helped architect the data flow and configuration system for dynamic content of the React UI. I contributed to the development of the React UI codebase and it’s architecture as the product has grown. I also led the CI/CD DevOps for the UI projects. This involved integrating with automatic testing solutions, building test cases, reporting, deployments, and quality checks. I also led the monitoring and alerting for the UI projects to monitor production and test performance and provide data to management.
