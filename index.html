What I do

Front End Development

I have a passion for developing responsive and effective front end experiences using modern technologies. I have collaborated with UX designers to create experiences that give users confidence and remove roadblocks. I believe that data collection on front end experiences is critical to measuring the success of the experience. My experience includes; React, Redux, styled-components, Sass, css, javascript, and HTML for building experiences. Jest, React Testing Library, Cypress, Axe, and Polaris for testing front end codebases. Splunk and Kibana for monitoring and alerting.

Back End Development

I believe that back end systems that are well designed are the cornerstone of a good user experience. Back end systems should be secure, reliable, efficient, and well documented. Monitoring, alerting, and testing these systems is critical to their performance. My experience includes; Node.js, Java, JBOSS, Springboot, and Node for building apis. JUnit, Mockito, Postman, and ReadyApi for testing apis. Splunk and Kibana for monitoring and alerting.

DevOps

DevOps should allow teams and developers to deploy, test, document, and deliver experiences as efficiently and reliably as possible. The DevOps systems should be an asset to the development teams and allow them to focus on coding and testing experiences with the confidence that deployments, automatic test runs, and documentation will be done reliably. My experience includes; GitLab CI/CD, OpenShift, Kubernetes, Docker, Artifactory, and qtest.
