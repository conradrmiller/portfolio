Creality Ender 3 V2

3D Printer

3D printing is one of my hobbies. My printer is a Creality Ender 3 V2. 3D printing allows me to be creative and have fun producing objects, crafts, robotics, toys, and prototypes. I also have had opportunities to develop my troubleshooting and tuning skills. There are so many systems involved with 3D printing that it can make troubleshooting complex. It forces you to isolate one variable at time and use effective tests to isolate the issue. I look forward to being able to continue to develop my 3d printing and modeling skills with the goal of being able to make custom robotics projects in the future.

I have made some modifications to my printers including: Octoprint running on a Raspberry Pi 4 in a 3d printed case, 3d printed mount for pi-camera monitoring, stiffer bed leveling springs, aluminum leveling knobs, PEI magnetic print bed, all metal extruder, upgraded Bowden tubbing, updated firmware, and 3d printed bearing spool holder.
